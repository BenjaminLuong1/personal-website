About Me

Hi, I'm Benjamin. I'm a part of the class of 2030 and going to Mills High School next year. I love being outside, whether it's hanging out with friends, playing sports like basketball or volleyball, or swimming to cool off.

Some of the key milestones of my life are playing for Ambassadors, a team supporting Christ, playing for my middle school team, and completing a civics and government class where I got to meet Mayor, Anders Fung. Some of my recent projects are making a slideshow and research paper about the risks of energy drinks for teens.
